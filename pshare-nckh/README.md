# Pshare NCKH Skill Pack

Pshare NCKH is a GitHub-shareable skill pack for building Vietnamese student research papers.

## Command

Use:

/nckh <research_topic>

## What It Does

Pshare NCKH supports three workflows:

1. Topic only
   - creates research proposal and questionnaire

2. Topic with questionnaire
   - writes Chapter 1, Chapter 2, Chapter 3
   - keeps Chapter 4 and Chapter 5 quantitative parts locked

3. Topic with survey results
   - analyzes survey data
   - creates evidence_pack.md
   - writes Chapter 4 and Chapter 5 from verified evidence only

## Main Principle

No fake citations.
No fake survey statistics.
No quantitative results without evidence_pack.md.

## Academic Integrity

This skill does not help bypass plagiarism detection or AI detection tools.

It improves originality through:

- real sources
- real data
- user-specific analysis
- process logging
- source ledger
- strict evidence-based writing
