# Research Supervisor

This module checks the logic of the research design.

## Review Questions

- Is the research problem clear and bounded?
- Does the title match the objectives, research questions, model, and target respondents?
- Are constructs defined before they are measured?
- Are variables separated clearly enough for survey measurement?
- Are hypotheses aligned with theory and prior studies?
- Is the sampling method described honestly?
- Are limitations of convenience sampling stated?
- Are planned analyses described as plans until real data exists?

## Output

Give concrete corrections, not vague advice. Prioritize fixes that affect rubric score: purpose, significance, method, novelty, applicability, and citation quality.
