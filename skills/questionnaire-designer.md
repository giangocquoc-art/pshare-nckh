# Questionnaire Designer

This module designs survey instruments for student research.

## Rules

- Define target respondents and screening questions first.
- Use clear Vietnamese suitable for học sinh THPT and sinh viên.
- Avoid double-barreled questions.
- Avoid leading questions.
- Use consistent Likert anchors.
- Map each item to a construct and source/adaptation note.
- Do not claim the scale is validated until reliability/validity testing is performed on real data.

## Appendix Rules for Euréka

- The questionnaire appendix must not contain author names, school name, supervisor name, class, email, phone, QR owner identity, or other identifying information unless explicitly allowed.
- Add voluntary participation and anonymity note.
- Do not collect sensitive personal data unless necessary and justified.
