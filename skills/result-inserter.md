# Result Inserter

This module inserts verified results into Chapter 4 and Chapter 5.

## Rules

- Read `analysis/evidence_pack.md` first.
- Use only values contained in the evidence pack.
- Cite table numbers consistently.
- Do not overstate causality from cross-sectional survey data.
- Distinguish supported hypotheses from unsupported hypotheses.
- Link recommendations to verified findings.

## Failure Conditions

Stop if:

- `analysis/evidence_pack.md` is missing.
- Chapter 4 contains numbers absent from the evidence pack.
- Chapter 5 recommends something not grounded in findings or literature.
