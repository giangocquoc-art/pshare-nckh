# Euréka Standards Module

Use this module whenever a manuscript is meant for Giải thưởng Sinh viên Nghiên cứu Khoa học Euréka.

## Presentation Standard

Baseline format:

- A4 paper.
- Times New Roman, 13 pt for main text.
- Line spacing 1.3–1.5.
- Margins: left 3 cm, top 2 cm, bottom 2 cm, right 2 cm.
- Page number centered at the top.
- Body paragraphs justified, first-line indent about 1.0–1.27 cm.
- No centered body paragraphs except cover/title/caption where appropriate.

## Required / Expected Structure

Use this order:

1. Cover page.
2. Table of contents.
3. List of tables.
4. List of figures/schemes/charts if any.
5. List of abbreviations if any.
6. Abstract / Tóm tắt công trình, maximum 1 A4 page.
7. Problem statement / Đặt vấn đề or Mô tả nghiên cứu.
8. Literature review / Tổng quan tài liệu / Cơ sở lý luận.
9. Objectives, methods, research design, sample, scales, data analysis plan.
10. Results and discussion.
11. Conclusion and recommendations.
12. References.
13. Appendices if any.

Do not include `Lời cảm ơn` by default. Do not include author/team/school/supervisor identity in the body, appendices, questionnaire, or metadata.

## Rubric Alignment

The paper should make grading easy:

- Purpose and significance: make the problem, gap, objectives, and contribution clear.
- Content, creativity, and method: align theory, model, hypotheses, scale, questionnaire, method, results, and discussion.
- Novelty: show what is new in topic, context, model, variables, scale adaptation, or practical angle. Do not overclaim.
- Applicability: connect findings/recommendations to practice only after evidence exists.
- Presentation: use complete citations, clear tables/figures, consistent formatting, and clean references.

## Red Flags to Fix

Fail or flag the manuscript if any of these appear in final output:

- `Cập nhật sau khi mở Word` placeholder remains.
- `[Cần bổ sung nguồn]` remains.
- Abstract exceeds 1 A4 page.
- Main text is centered.
- Margins do not match left 3 cm/top-bottom-right 2 cm.
- Line spacing is 1.0.
- Blank grids/tables are used to force layout.
- Tables/figures are not numbered or not captioned.
- References are incomplete or not cited in text.
- Chapter 4/5 contain statistical findings without real data.
- Any identity leak appears in manuscript body, appendix, questionnaire, comments, properties, or hidden metadata.
