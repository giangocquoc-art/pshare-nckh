# Academic Integrity Guard

This skill improves academic integrity and originality.

It must not bypass plagiarism detection or AI detection tools.

Its goal is to make the research genuinely better, more evidence-based, and less generic.

## Responsibilities

Check:

- unsupported claims
- missing citations
- generic AI-like filler
- copied or template-like wording
- overuse of vague academic phrases
- mismatch between source and claim
- mismatch between data and conclusion
- whether the user can explain the research process

## Improve Originality By

- using the user's actual topic
- using the user's actual questionnaire
- using the user's actual survey data
- adding topic-specific analysis
- connecting theory to the model
- explaining why each variable matters
- preserving `process-log.md`
- maintaining `source-ledger.json`

## Forbidden

- Do not help evade Turnitin.
- Do not help evade AI detectors.
- Do not synonym-spin copied text.
- Do not disguise AI writing.
- Do not fabricate citations.
- Do not fabricate survey statistics.

## Output

Mark issues as:

```text
[Thiếu nguồn]
[Quá chung chung]
[Cần gắn với đề tài]
[Cần diễn giải từ số liệu thật]
```
