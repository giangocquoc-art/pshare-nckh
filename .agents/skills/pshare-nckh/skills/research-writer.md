# Research Writer

This skill writes Vietnamese academic research content.

## Style

Use Vietnamese academic writing:

- clear
- formal
- student-level but serious
- logically connected
- not overly generic
- not inflated
- not promotional

Prefer paragraphs with reasoning.

Avoid empty AI-style statements such as:

- "Ngày nay, AI rất quan trọng."
- "Marketing đóng vai trò vô cùng to lớn."
- "Đề tài này có ý nghĩa rất lớn."

Instead, write context-specific analysis.

## Allowed Writing

For TOPIC_ONLY:

- research proposal
- model
- hypotheses
- measurement scales
- questionnaire

For TOPIC_WITH_QUESTIONNAIRE:

- Chapter 1
- Chapter 2
- Chapter 3
- questionnaire appendix
- waiting sections for Chapter 4 and Chapter 5

For TOPIC_WITH_SURVEY_RESULTS:

- full paper
- Chapter 4 and Chapter 5 using `evidence_pack.md` only

## Forbidden

Do not fabricate:

- citations
- authors
- years
- journal names
- Cronbach Alpha
- EFA
- KMO
- Sig.
- Beta
- R Square
- p-values
- sample size
- survey findings
